Business Name Generator

Generate a short, brand able business name using artificial intelligence.

How to Use a Business Name Generator

Get name ideas

We create short, punchy, memorable brand names using a combination of your keywords and artificial intelligence. The more specific you are, the better the result.

Build a Brand

The perfect name is a great first step. Use it as a foundation for your entire brand and approach to get recognition across all your marketing channels.

Create a Visual Identity

Our Business Name Generator creates results in a wide variety of colors, fonts and backgrounds. Use these as inspiration for the ideal design that represents your brand.

Why Choosing the Right Business
Name Is Important

Your business identity, or brand, shapes how customers see you
and influences their decision to buy. A short, distinctive
name shows confidence and creates excitement around your products and
services. Our Business Name Generator will help you create unforgettable,
unique, simple business names that showcase your business values in the best
possible way.

How to Brainstorm Company Names

The Business Name Generator uses the keywords you provide us with,
then sprinkles on a little AI magic to create dozens of possible business names.
You can start simply, with just one or two keywords, and then
enter additional words to fine-tune your results. There are plenty of ways
to create the initial keywords. Brainstorm words associated with:

The specific niche for your business

The types of products and services you offer

Who your ideal audience is

The goals you have for your business
For more inspiration, use a thesaurus to find and include similar words.
