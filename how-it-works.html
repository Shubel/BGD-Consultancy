How It Works

LLC formation, registered agent, compliance, and startup services — all in one place. Start your business today for $0 + state fee. Doing the busy work so you can build your big ideas. Here's how it works...

3 Easy Steps

1

Find the Right Entity for You

Select an entity type and the preferred state of incorporation. Need help with state-specific formation details? Check out our LLC State Guides or Corporation State Guides.

2

Select a Package

See our package options below and choose one that best suits your business needs, whether you're looking for the bare basics or more robust support. No matter what formation package you select, BGD Consultancy will be there to help you as your business grows.

3

Tell Us About Your Business

Complete the online order forms and provide information about your business and the services that will best suit your business.

We've Received Your Order.

What's Next?

You get access to your own personal business dashboard. From there, you can review all of your order details to ensure everything is just as it should be.

A thorough name search is conducted with the state to ensure your company name is unique to you and not already taken. If you’d like to do a search for your business name now, you can use our Business Name Search tool.

Articles of Incorporation (Corporations) / Organization (LLCs) are prepared and delivered to the governing state agency. We got you.

The filed articles along with any additional services and documents are accessible from your custom business dashboard. You’ll receive notification once they’re ready. All set!

But wait — your business dashboard is not just a dashboard to access your incorporation documents. We’ve worked hard to leverage our technology team to build a useful, one-stop place for all your business needs.
